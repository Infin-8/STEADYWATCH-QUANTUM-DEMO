# Privacy Policy
## SteadyWatch - Precision Hand Stability Monitor

**Effective Date:** December 12, 2025

## Introduction

SteadyWatch ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SteadyWatch (the "App") on Apple Watch and iPhone.

**By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Motion and Sensor Data

**What We Collect:**
- Hand stability measurements from Apple Watch motion sensors
- Acceleration data (x, y, z axes)
- Gyroscope data
- Wrist angle measurements (extension and deviation)
- Tremor detection data
- Precision session metrics

**How We Use It:**
- To measure and display your hand stability in real-time
- To provide precision scores and feedback
- To track your progress over time
- To enable procedure-specific monitoring (surgery, dental, engineering, etc.)

**Storage:**
- All motion data is stored **locally on your device**
- Data is stored using Apple's UserDefaults and Core Data
- **We do not transmit motion data to our servers**

---

### 2. Health Data (Optional)

**What We Collect (if you grant permission):**
- Heart rate data (from Apple Watch)
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Workout session data

**How We Use It:**
- To provide stress indicators
- To assess autonomic nervous system state
- To enhance precision monitoring with physiological context

**Storage:**
- Health data is managed through Apple's HealthKit framework
- Health data remains on your device and in your iCloud account (if enabled)
- **We do not access or store health data on our servers**

**Your Control:**
- You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
- You can delete health data through the Health app

---

### 3. App Usage Data

**What We Collect:**
- Procedure type selections (surgery, dental, engineering, etc.)
- Session history and timestamps
- User preferences and settings
- App configuration choices

**How We Use It:**
- To personalize your experience
- To maintain your settings across app launches
- To provide historical data and trends

**Storage:**
- All usage data is stored **locally on your device**
- **We do not transmit usage data to our servers**

---

### 4. Network Communication

**What We Collect:**
- API requests to backend services (if configured)
- Connection status and error logs

**How We Use It:**
- To enable quantum visualization features (optional)
- To sync with backend services (if you configure a backend URL)
- To provide real-time quantum computing features

**Storage:**
- Network logs are stored **locally on your device** only
- API requests are made to servers you configure (localhost or your own server)
- **We do not collect or store network data on our servers**

**Your Control:**
- Backend connection is **optional** and **user-configured**
- You can disable backend features in app settings
- Default configuration uses localhost (no external communication)

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device:**
- Motion data: Stored in UserDefaults and Core Data
- Health data: Managed by Apple's HealthKit (stored in Health app)
- Session history: Stored locally on your device
- Settings: Stored in UserDefaults

**Data Security:**
- All local data is protected by iOS/watchOS security features
- HealthKit data is encrypted by Apple
- We use Apple's standard security frameworks

### Cloud Storage (Optional)

**If you enable iCloud:**
- Health data may sync to your iCloud account (Apple's service)
- This is controlled by your iCloud settings, not by our app
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Share Your Data

**We do not:**
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Share your data with analytics companies
- ❌ Share your data with other apps or services
- ❌ Transmit your data to our servers

**All data remains on your device.**

### Backend Services (User-Configured)

**If you configure a backend URL:**
- The app may send API requests to servers you specify
- This is **your choice** and **your responsibility**
- We do not control or access these servers
- Data sent to your configured backend is subject to that server's privacy policy

**Default Configuration:**
- Default backend URL is `localhost` (no external communication)
- No data leaves your device unless you configure a custom backend

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data

**You can:**
- View all session history within the app
- Export data to CSV or PDF formats
- Access HealthKit data through Apple's Health app

### Delete Your Data

**You can:**
- Delete individual sessions from within the app
- Clear all app data by deleting and reinstalling the app
- Delete HealthKit data through the Health app
- Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings

### Control Data Collection

**You can:**
- Disable HealthKit permissions (app will work without health data)
- Disable backend features (use app in offline mode)
- Adjust motion sensor permissions in iOS Settings
